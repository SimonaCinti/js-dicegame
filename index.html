How to play

On their turn, a player rolls the dice. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible, adding up the numbers rolled on the dice. But if a player rolls a 1, the player's turn is over and any points they have gained during this turn are lost, but not the total score obtained during the game. Rolling a 1 doesn't wipe out your entire score

A player can also choose to hold if they do not want to risk to roll the dice again. If the player chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to their total score.

When a player reaches a total of 100 or more points, the game ends and that player is the winner.
